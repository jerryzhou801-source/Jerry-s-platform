Canola/China: GAC approved Australian rapeseed into designated Chinese crushers; ZCE-implied crush margins are positive on Australian, negative on Canadian, with buying shifting Australian from November. Chinese demand should lift Australian prices and cut their competitiveness into Europe — tightening the EU's marginal import leg just as Canada's crop lags badly (Saskatchewan 27% flowering vs 55% LY). Structurally supportive for MATIF and the European import premium.
