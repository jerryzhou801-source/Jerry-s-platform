Rapeseed/RSO: New-crop RSO rose (ASO +€10.50 to €1,152/t) on Euronext strength; French 40°C heat has limited impact with harvest underway, Germany starts next week. StatCan lifted Canadian acreage 8% and Argus sees record 22.7mn t output, but exports fall 500kt to 8mn t on higher domestic crush — and development lags badly (Alberta only 7% flowering vs 21% avg), keeping the July flowering window the key swing factor. Short-term firm; medium-term hinges on Canadian weather.

Sunflower/EU vegoils: Argus cut EU 2026-27 SFS by 500kt to 9.85mn t on heat damage, shifting incremental demand toward rapeseed, while global SFS output rebounds +6.8mn t to 62.1mn t — spot SFO firm ($1,472.50) but OND fell $62.50 to $1,345. Turkey tied new import quotas to domestic buying, and China's import growth may pivot to SFO, with ZCE SFO futures possibly launching in H2 2026.

Global/SBO/Palm: CBOT soybeans surged on US heat (Brazil Aug premium -10¢ to +96¢); Indonesian palm output is projected to decline through 2027 on aging trees and B50, keeping palm elevated and supporting the wider vegoil complex. Watch WASDE/MPOB 10 July; Euronext rapeseed options expire 15 July.
